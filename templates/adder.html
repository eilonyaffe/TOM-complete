The OSINT Matrix

An information preservation framework
An Input/ Output based system, for reaching the tools you need and sharing tools with colleagues


Adding a Tool to the framework

  • Name: enter the name in lower case, without . or , and without spaces (hunterio not hunter.io) / (social_bearings not social bearings)
  • Photo: give a URL (including https://) to a simple rectangular photo. open Google images, right-click on the desired image, choose "open image in a new tab", and copy that URL
  • Link: the URL of the website (including https://), preferably its homepage or most relevant URL.
  • Date: the date of today, in the format of DD/MM/YYYY

Choose the corresponding input/output setting

Always assign the same input/output in one of the assignments below (for example- FB profile, FB profile), so it can be seen amongst all other tools
